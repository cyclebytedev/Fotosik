# Privacy Policy for Photo Spot Note

Last updated: 4 July 2026

Photo Spot Note is a local-first app for saving photography locations, notes, photos, audio notes, and planned visits.

This privacy policy explains what information the app uses, where it is stored, and when information may leave the app through optional system features.

## Data Stored by the App

Photo Spot Note may store the following information when you create a photo spot:

- Spot name
- Text notes
- Map coordinates
- Planned visit date
- Visited status
- Reference photo
- Audio note
- Calendar event identifier, if you choose to add a planned visit to Calendar

This data is stored locally on your device inside the app’s private storage.

## No Tracking or Analytics

Photo Spot Note does not collect analytics, tracking data, advertising identifiers, or account information.

The app does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

The app does not operate a server and does not send your photo spot data to us.

## Location

Photo Spot Note uses location permission only to help you save your current location as a photo spot and show your position on the map.

You can also place a spot manually on the map without using your current GPS location.

Your saved spot coordinates are stored locally in the app unless you choose to share them through Calendar using the Full Details option.

## Photos

If you take or choose a reference photo, the app stores a processed copy locally inside the app.

Photo Spot Note reprocesses saved photos to reduce file size and remove original image metadata, including embedded EXIF/GPS metadata.

The app does not upload your photos.

## Audio Notes

If you record an audio note, the recording is stored locally inside the app.

The app does not upload your audio recordings.

## Calendar Integration

Calendar integration is optional.

If you choose to add a planned visit to Calendar, Photo Spot Note creates a Calendar event on your device.

Calendar events may sync through the Calendar account configured on your device, such as iCloud, Google, or Exchange. That syncing is managed by your Calendar provider, not by Photo Spot Note.

Photo Spot Note offers two Calendar detail levels:

- Private: the Calendar event includes the spot title and an app link only.
- Full Details: the Calendar event also includes your note and exact map location.

You can choose the level of detail before adding a Calendar event.

## Apple Maps

Photo Spot Note uses Apple Maps / MapKit to display maps and open saved locations in Apple Maps.

Use of Apple Maps is subject to Apple’s own terms and privacy practices.

## Backups and Local Storage

Photo Spot Note is designed to keep your data local to your device.

For privacy, photo and audio attachment folders are excluded from system backups where supported.

This means deleting the app, losing the device, or replacing the device may remove locally stored spot media unless you have another copy.

## Deleting Your Data

You can delete individual photo spots inside the app.

Deleting a spot removes its local photo and audio attachments from the app.

If a spot has a linked Calendar event, the app will attempt to remove that Calendar event when the spot is deleted.

You can also delete all app data by deleting Photo Spot Note from your device.

## Children’s Privacy

Photo Spot Note is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

The app does not provide accounts, messaging, advertising, or tracking.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app’s features change.

If future versions add features such as iCloud sync, export/import, or notifications, this policy will be updated to explain how those features handle data.
